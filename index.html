Minor Axis

Minor Axis is an independent technical investigation and reconciliation practice.

We work on financial and operational systems where reports, transactions, APIs, dashboards, or data pipelines may disagree. We trace how records are produced, where discrepancies enter, and what can be reconstructed from the available evidence.

Areas of interest include financial record reconstruction, reporting consistency, infrastructure debugging, and data reliability. Experience includes portfolio simulations, financial risk modelling, derivatives pricing, reporting systems, and software development
